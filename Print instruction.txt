3D print instruction

Our Motor TCD prototype was printed unsing Utlimaker S5.
Hence, the following settings are the settings that we use in Ultimaker Cura
slicer version 5.1.1

Print material: 
- Primary (to use for printing of part): Ultimaker PLA Magenta, print core AA 0.4
- Secondary (to use for printing the support and raft):  
Resoluion: 0.1mm
infill: 20%
Support: on (printed with dissovable material)
Adhesion: on

Total number of layers: 129
Total material used: 53g - 6.72 m
Time for the print to complete: 11 hours 54 minutes
